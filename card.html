HORSE

Horses have near 360 degree vision.

  • Scientific Name: Equus caballus
  • Average Lifespan: 25-30
  • Average Height: (1.4-1.8)m
  • Habitat: Flexible

The horse is one of the most captivating mammal to walk the earth. It is said, they have been evolving since 50 million years from species much smaller in size. They can gallop at an average speed of around 44kmph. The are known have have been domesticated since 5000 years, which proves them as one of the most loyal friend to the human species.
